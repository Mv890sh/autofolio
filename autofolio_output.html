AutoFolio — Project Structure & Dependencies
📄 main.dart
App entry point. Initializes Hive, opens 'vehicles' box, runs AutoFolioApp with MaterialApp → HomePage.
🚗 models/vehicle.dart
Vehicle data model with: name, type, totalDistance, fuelEfficiency fields.
🏠 screens/home_page.dart
StatefulWidget. Scaffold with AppBar, Drawer (UserAccountsDrawerHeader + ListTiles), Card, FAB.
⛽ screens/fuel_entry_screen.dart
Form screen with GlobalKey, 3x TextFormField (odometer, litres, cost), ElevatedButton to save & pop.
🔧 screens/service_reminder_screen.dart
StatelessWidget. ListView with 3 Cards: Oil Change, Insurance Renewal, Tyre Replacement.
📦 pubspec.yaml — Dependencies
hive: ^2.2.3 hive_flutter: ^1.1.0 fl_chart: ^0.68.0 path_provider: ^2.0.15 hive_generator: ^2.0.1 build_runner: ^2.4.6
